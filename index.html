About Dr. Soje

Healing Minds,
Restoring Lives

Dr. Mrs. Anthonia Yemisi Soje is a Nigerian medical practitioner and mental health professional who has dedicated her career to the holistic restoration of individuals and families. As Founder & CEO of Fosterheirs Holistic Mental Health Therapists, she serves as a bridge between clinical medicine, psychological therapy, and faith-based healing.

Her approach is distinctively integrative — medicine, psychology, spirituality, and community rehabilitation woven together. She works with individuals battling addiction, trauma, ADHD, relationship crises, and emotional instability, offering a compassionate, evidence-informed, and faith-anchored path to recovery.

The Organization

Fosterheirs Holistic
Mental Health Therapists

Founded in 2023 by Dr. Soje, Fosterheirs is a counseling and rehabilitation organization based in Oye-Ekiti, Nigeria. Nestled within the Heirs Specialist Hospital Complex, it operates as a sanctuary for holistic healing — addressing addiction, trauma, marital crises, and emotional instability.

The organization's mission is a fourfold healing approach: medical intervention, psychological therapy, social rehabilitation, and spiritual anchoring — because true recovery honors the whole person.
